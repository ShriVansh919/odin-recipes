Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer where chunks of paneer are marinated in spiced yogurt and grilled or roasted until smoky and charred.


Ingredients:

  1. Paneer cubes
  2. Thick curd (yogurt)
  3. Ginger garlic paste
  4. Red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala
  5. Chaat masala
  6. Capsicum and onion cubes
  7. Salt
  8. Oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix curd with spices, salt, and ginger garlic paste to make a marinade.
  2. Coat paneer, onions, and capsicum in the marinade. Rest for at least 30 mins.
  3. Skewer and grill or pan-roast until charred and cooked.
  4. Sprinkle with chaat masala and serve hot with mint chutney.
