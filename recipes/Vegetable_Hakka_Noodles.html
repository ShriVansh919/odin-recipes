Vegetable Hakka Noodles

Indo-Chinese favorite made with stir-fried noodles and vegetables tossed in soy sauce and spices.


Ingredients:

  1. Boiled Hakka noodles
  2. Julienned vegetables (carrot, cabbage, bell peppers)
  3. Soy sauce
  4. Green chilies
  5. Ginger garlic paste
  6. Spring onions
  7. Oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a wok and add garlic-ginger paste, saut√© briefly.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry on high heat.
  3. Add noodles and soy sauce, toss everything well.
  4. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
