Litti Chokha

A rustic Bihari delicacy made with roasted wheat balls stuffed with spiced gram flour, served with mashed veggies.


Ingredients:

  1. Wheat flour
  2. Sattu (roasted gram flour)
  3. Garlic, ginger, green chilies
  4. Pickle masala
  5. Mustard oil
  6. Brinjal, tomatoes, boiled potatoes
  7. Spices and salt

Instructions:

  1. Prepare dough and stuff with sattu masala mixture.
  2. Roast on coal or bake until browned and smoky.
  3. Chokha: Roast veggies, mash with mustard oil, garlic, and spices.
  4. Serve litti hot with chokha and extra ghee.
