Aloo Paratha

Aloo Tikki is a popular snack made with boiled and mashed potatoes, spices, and herbs, shallo w-fried until crisp.


Ingredients:

  1. Boiled potatoes
  2. Spices (like cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  3. Chopped coriander leaves
  4. Green chilies
  5. Salt
  6. Oil for frying
  7. Pinterest
  8. Dassana's Veg Recipes

Instructions:

  1. Mash the boiled potatoes and mix with spices, chopped coriander, green chilies, and salt.
  2. Shape into patties.
  3. Shallow fry until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
